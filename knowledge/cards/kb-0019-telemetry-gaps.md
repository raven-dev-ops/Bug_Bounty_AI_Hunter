## Summary
Caching and event coverage choices can create blind spots where events are not observed or are skipped.

## Relevance to the project
Guides validation of telemetry coverage and cache behavior.

## Safe notes
- Map coverage for all critical event types.
- Validate cache behavior and invalidation paths.
- Monitor for dropped or skipped events.
- Review performance trade-offs against visibility needs.
